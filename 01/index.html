Jeanelle's Lessons From 2019

First Lesson

There are people who are very generous even to those who are not related to them. It is important to share what you have. When I am successful, I want to be like them and give back to the people who supported us along the way.

Second Lesson

I learned that being involved in this kind of organization helps me conquer my shyness and collaboration skills. It is fulfilling to complete tasks and learning the ins and outs of the organization.

Third Lesson

In studies, there are trials where you need to be strong. There are times we feel heavily burdened. Learn how to step up and rise from your failures.

Fourth Lesson

Stay focused. They said it is the key in achieving your goals.

Fifth Lesson

There are trials we cannot avoid. Life is not all happy moments. Sometimes there are, things we don’t like to happen, like passing of a loved one. No matter how much we plan for the things we want, life will throw whatever it wants. The trial will still be given to you even if it’s the one you fear the most. Failures and hardships like this must become stepping stones towards a better version of yourself.
